Kenai Fjords

Kenai Fjords National Park is located in southern Alaska, near the town of Seward. It's known for its landscapes, featuring glaciers, coastal rainforests, rugged mountains, and abundant wildlife. The park is renowned for its fantasic fjords, which were carved by glaciers, and the Harding Icefield(one of the largest icefields in the U.S). Visitors can explore the park through boat tours, hiking trails, or kayaking, with opportunities to see sea otters, humpback whales, puffins, and even black bears. The park is a popular spot for outdoor activities like kayaking, fishing, and wildlife viewing.
